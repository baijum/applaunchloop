Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 24, 2026

AppLaunchLoop is a free and open source Android utility built by Baijum. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the app.

Summary

AppLaunchLoop is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect personal data, we do not track you, and we do not serve advertisements. All daily testing operations run entirely on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or account information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not serve advertisements.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

AppLaunchLoop stores the following data locally on your device using Android Jetpack DataStore. This data never leaves your device:

  • Active campaign ID
  • Target app package name(s)
  • Daily testing streak count (0–14)
  • Timestamp of last test run
  • Your role preference (Creator or Tester)
  • Campaign IDs you have created

You can clear all local data at any time by clearing the app's data in Android Settings or by uninstalling the app.

Data Stored in the Cloud

When a Creator creates a testing campaign, the following information is written to Firebase Firestore:

  • A randomly generated campaign ID
  • The Google Group email address entered by the Creator
  • The target app package name entered by the Creator
  • A timestamp of when the campaign was created
  • An anonymous device identifier (Android ID) so the Creator can manage or delete the campaign from their device

No user authentication or login is required. No personal information is stored in the cloud.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet & Network State — To read and write campaign data from Firebase Firestore.
  • Notifications (Android 13+) — To send daily testing reminder notifications. You can deny or revoke this permission at any time.
  • Boot Completed — To reschedule daily reminder notifications after a device restart.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Firestore (by Google) to store campaign data. Firebase may collect limited technical data such as IP addresses for service operation. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. The source code and this policy are publicly available at github.com/baijum/applaunchloop.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
